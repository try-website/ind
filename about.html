About us

Our King Universe Matriculation higher secondary school was co-founded by Nallasiriyar (Late) Dr. T. Raja Muthukumar along with our present correspondent Mrs.Muthu Krishnaveni P in the year 1996 with 40 students, 4 teachers and 1 Principal in a rental building at Krishnapuram, Kadayanallur. The School runs under  RMK Educational Trust. From the rental building at Krishnapuram, our school was shifted to massive and imposing buildings with architectural splendor on Madurai Road, Kumanthapuram, Kadayanallur. Our School is surrounded by shady trees, beautiful gardens, and multiple fruit-yielding trees which will give you a warm welcome.Now Our School is working as a full-fledged and recognized Matriculation higher secondary school. Apart from the curricular activities, our school imparts extracurricular activities like yoga, karate, classical dance, skating, chess, etc.,
